<p><p>The data logger market is categorized into three main types: mechanical, electronic, and wireless data loggers. Mechanical data loggers utilize analog methods, often relying on physical changes to record data, making them simple yet limited in features. Electronic data loggers employ digital technology for more precise data collection and often include advanced functionalities, such as display screens and programmable settings. Wireless data loggers offer real-time data transmission via Wi-Fi or cellular networks, enhancing convenience and accessibility for remote monitoring and analysis.</p></p>
