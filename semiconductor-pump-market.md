<p><p>The semiconductor pump market includes various types essential for creating vacuum conditions in semiconductor manufacturing. Dry pumps are oil-free and suitable for clean applications. Multi-stage roots pumps provide high throughput and are efficient for large volumes. Turbomolecular pumps utilize high speeds for low pressures, making them ideal for ultra-high vacuum environments. Claw pumps deliver a robust, maintenance-free solution with low vibration. Other pumps may include hybrid or specialized designs, catering to specific needs in the semiconductor production process.</p></p>
