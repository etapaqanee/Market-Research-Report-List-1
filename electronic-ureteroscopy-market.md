<p><p>The electronic ureteroscopy market is divided into two main types: single-use ureteroscopes and reusable ureteroscopes. Single-use ureteroscopes are designed for one-time use, offering enhanced sterility and eliminating the need for reprocessing, thus reducing infection risks. In contrast, reusable ureteroscopes are durable instruments meant for multiple procedures, providing cost-effectiveness over time but requiring stringent sterilization protocols. Both types cater to the growing demand for minimally invasive procedures and technological advancements in diagnosing and treating urinary tract conditions.</p></p>
