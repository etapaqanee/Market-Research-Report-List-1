<p><p>The sintering diamond saw blades market is segmented into dry and wet operation types, each catering to specific cutting needs. Dry operation saw blades are designed for tasks where water cooling is impractical, offering portability and convenience, but may generate more dust and heat. In contrast, wet operation saw blades utilize water to reduce heat and enhance cutting performance, making them ideal for materials that require cooling, such as concrete and stone, resulting in smoother cuts and prolonged blade life.</p></p>
