<p><p>Property Management Software Platforms primarily fall into two market types: cloud-based and on-premises. Cloud-based solutions are hosted on remote servers, allowing users to access the software from anywhere via the internet. This model offers scalability, automatic updates, and reduced IT costs. Conversely, on-premises software is installed directly on a company's local servers, providing greater control over data security and customization. However, it typically requires higher upfront costs and maintenance efforts, making it less flexible than cloud-based options.</p></p>
