<p><ul><li>Two-High Rolling Mills</li><li>Four-High Rolling Mills</li><li>Six-High Rolling Mills</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Strip rolling mills are specialized machinery used in metal processing, available in various configurations to suit different production needs. Two-high rolling mills consist of two opposing rolls, ideal for smaller operations with simpler requirements. Four-high rolling mills feature four rolls, enhancing control and reducing thickness more effectively, ideal for high-capacity production. Six-high rolling mills add two additional rolls, allowing for even thinner strips and better handling of materials, making them suitable for advanced applications in the automotive and aerospace industries.</p></p>
