<p><p>Digital insulation testers measure the resistance of insulation materials in electrical systems, ensuring safety and reliability. The market is segmented into two main types: AC voltage testers and DC voltage testers. AC voltage testers apply alternating current to assess insulation integrity, useful for checking live circuits. Conversely, DC voltage testers utilize direct current, providing a stable measurement for high-resistance insulation in cables and equipment. Each type serves specific applications based on the requirements of the electrical systems being tested.</p></p>
