<p><p>A Marine Steam Turbine is a type of turbine used in marine propulsion systems, primarily in large vessels such as oil tankers, LNG carriers, and naval ships. These turbines utilize steam generated from water heated by boilers to drive the turbine and propel the vessel. The efficiency and reliability of marine steam turbines make them a preferred choice in certain applications despite the rise of alternative propulsion technologies.</p><p>The Marine Steam Turbine Market is expected to grow at a CAGR of 6.6% during the forecast period. Additionally, the rise in offshore activities and naval modernization programs presents new opportunities for market expansion.</p><p>Recent trends in the market include the development of hybrid propulsion systems that combine steam turbines with other forms of energy generation, such as diesel or gas turbines.
