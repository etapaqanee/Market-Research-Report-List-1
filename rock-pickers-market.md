<p><ul><li>Trailed</li><li>Mounted</li><li>Semi-mounted</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Rock pickers are essential agricultural implements used for removing stones from fields. They come in three main types: trailed, mounted, and semi-mounted. Trailed models are towed behind tractors, offering flexibility and ease of transport. Mounted rock pickers attach directly to the tractor, providing better maneuverability for tight spaces. Semi-mounted designs combine features of both, offering stability and improved weight distribution while allowing for greater mobility. Each type is designed to enhance efficiency in stone removal, tailored to different farming needs.</p></p>
