<p><strong>The Surgical Navigation Software Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Optical</li><li>Electromagnetic (EM)</li><li>Hybrid</li></ul></p>
<p><p>Surgical navigation software is categorized into three main types: optical, electromagnetic (EM), and hybrid systems. Optical navigation utilizes cameras and reflective markers to track instruments relative to the patient’s anatomy, offering high precision in applications like orthopedic surgeries. Electromagnetic navigation employs magnetic fields to track the position of instruments, allowing for flexibility in movement without line of sight constraints. Hybrid systems combine both optical and electromagnetic technologies, enhancing tracking capabilities and improving surgical accuracy, adaptability, and overall outcomes.</p></p>
