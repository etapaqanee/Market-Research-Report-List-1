<p><p>Transparent PVC films and sheets are produced using various technologies, including calendering, extrusion, and laminating. Calendering involves passing PVC through rollers to create thin sheets with uniform thickness. Extrusion technology melts PVC and forms continuous sheets, offering versatility in thickness and width. Laminating combines multiple materials, enhancing strength and aesthetic appeal. Each method has unique advantages, catering to diverse applications in packaging, construction, and consumer goods, by providing clarity, flexibility, and durability in final products.</p></p>
