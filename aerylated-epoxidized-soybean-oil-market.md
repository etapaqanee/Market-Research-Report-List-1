<p><p>The Aerylated Epoxidized Soybean Oil market can be categorized based on production methods: Ring Opening Polymerization and Reaction with Maleic Anhydride. In Ring Opening Polymerization, the epoxidized oil undergoes a chemical reaction that opens the epoxy rings, leading to the creation of polymeric materials with enhanced characteristics. The Reaction with Maleic Anhydride involves the interaction between epoxidized soybean oil and maleic anhydride, resulting in modified oil with improved adhesion and stability. Both processes yield products suitable for various industrial applications.</p></p>
