<p><ul><li>Rigid Closed Cell Injection Foam</li><li>Open Cell Spray Foam</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The polyurethane spray foam market comprises two primary types: rigid closed cell injection foam and open cell spray foam. Rigid closed cell foam features a dense structure that provides excellent insulation, moisture resistance, and structural support, making it ideal for roofing and commercial applications. In contrast, open cell spray foam has a softer, less dense composition, allowing it to expand and fill gaps effectively, providing soundproofing and insulation in residential settings. Each type serves unique applications based on specific insulation needs.</p></p>
