<p><p>Refrigeration compressors in the light commercial market are essential for cooling systems. Reciprocating compressors use pistons for gas compression, ideal for small applications. Rotary compressors utilize rotating elements for a compact design, suitable for moderate cooling needs. Screw compressors employ two interlocking screws for efficient, continuous operation, often used in larger setups. Scroll compressors feature spiral elements for smooth, quiet operation, and are energy-efficient. Centrifugal compressors leverage high-speed rotors for large volume movement, typically utilized in expansive commercial facilities.</p></p>
