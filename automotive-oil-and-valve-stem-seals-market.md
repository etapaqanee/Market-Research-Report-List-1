<p><p>The automotive oil and valve stem seals market encompasses two key components. They are critical in areas where shafts enter the engine, such as crankshafts and camshafts. Valve stem seals, on the other hand, control oil flow to the engine's combustion chamber, preventing excessive oil consumption and reducing emissions. Together, these seals play essential roles in engine efficiency, longevity, and environmental compliance.</p></p>
