<p><strong>The Handheld Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) Device Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Spectral-Domain OCT</li><li>Swept-Source OCT</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The handheld Optical Coherence Tomography (OCT) device market primarily consists of two types: Spectral-Domain OCT (SD-OCT) and Swept-Source OCT (SS-OCT). SD-OCT utilizes a broad-spectrum light source and a spectrometer to obtain high-resolution images, making it ideal for applications requiring detailed structural analysis. In contrast, SS-OCT employs a tunable laser and delivers faster imaging speeds, enabling deeper tissue penetration and enhanced imaging of dynamic processes. Both technologies enhance diagnostic capabilities, particularly in ophthalmology and other medical fields.</p></p>
