<p><strong>The RFID Tag Chips Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>HF RFID Chip</li><li>UHF RFID Chip</li><li>LF RFID Chip</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The RFID tag chip market is categorized into three main types: HF (High Frequency) RFID chips, UHF (Ultra High Frequency) RFID chips, and LF (Low Frequency) RFID chips. HF RFID chips operate at 13.56 MHz, suitable for applications like access control and public transportation. UHF RFID chips, operating between 300 MHz and 3 GHz, are ideal for supply chain management and inventory tracking due to their longer read ranges. LF RFID chips, typically at 125-134 kHz, are often used in animal tagging and access systems, where proximity reading is essential.</p></p>
