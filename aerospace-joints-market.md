<p><p>The aerospace joints market encompasses various types, including gimbal joints, ball joints, and swivel joints. Gimbal joints allow for multi-directional movement, providing stability in applications like navigation systems. Ball joints facilitate rotational motion in multiple axes, crucial for connection between components. Swivel joints permit rotational movement around a fixed point, commonly used in fuel and hydraulic systems. Other types may include universal joints and hinge joints. Each type plays a vital role in ensuring the performance and reliability of aerospace systems.</p></p>
