<p><p>The forged steel gate valves market is segmented into three main types: bolted bonnet, welded bonnet, and pressure seal bonnet. </p><p>Bolted bonnet valves feature a removable cover secured with bolts, allowing for easy maintenance. Welded bonnet valves are permanently sealed, providing a robust and compact design, ideal for high-pressure applications. Pressure seal bonnet valves utilize the pressure of the medium to create a seal, ensuring minimal leakage and offering enhanced strength. Each type caters to specific industry requirements based on performance and application needs.</p></p>
