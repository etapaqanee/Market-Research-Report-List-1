<p><p>The blown rapeseed oil market includes types such as emulsifiable rapeseed oil and oxidized blown rapeseed oil. Emulsifiable rapeseed oil is refined to create a stable emulsion, making it suitable for food and cosmetic applications, enhancing texture and quality. In contrast, oxidized blown rapeseed oil undergoes controlled oxidation, enhancing its stability and shelf life, commonly used in coatings and industrial applications. Both types serve different sectors, leveraging the unique properties derived from the blowing process to meet specific market needs.</p></p>
