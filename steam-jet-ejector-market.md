<p><p>The Steam Jet Ejector market is divided into two main types: Single-Stage and Multi-Stage Steam Jet Ejectors. Single-Stage Steam Jet Ejectors efficiently handle low vacuum applications, utilizing steam to create a vacuum or move gases in a straightforward system design. In contrast, Multi-Stage Steam Jet Ejectors are designed for higher vacuum levels, employing a series of ejectors to progressively reduce pressure. This multi-stage approach enhances efficiency and effectiveness in complex applications requiring higher performance and controlled conditions.</p></p>
