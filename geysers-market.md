<p><p>The geyser market is segmented into three main types: non-electric geysers, gas geysers, and electric geysers. Non-electric geysers rely on solar energy or thermal heating methods, making them eco-friendly. Gas geysers are powered by natural gas or propane, offering quick heating options and lower operational costs but requiring proper ventilation. Electric geysers are convenient and widely used, heating water through electrical resistance, providing consistent hot water supply. Each type caters to different user preferences and energy efficiency needs.</p></p>
