<p><ul><li>"Internally Controlled Type"</li><li>"Externally Controlled Type"</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The variable displacement compressor market is categorized into two main types: internally controlled and externally controlled compressors. Internally controlled types adjust their displacement based on internal mechanisms, such as changing the angle of the swash plate, ensuring efficiency as demand fluctuates. Externally controlled types rely on external signals or controls, like electronic systems or pressure sensors, to optimize performance. Both types are essential for improving energy efficiency and performance in applications like automotive air conditioning and industrial refrigeration systems.</p></p>
