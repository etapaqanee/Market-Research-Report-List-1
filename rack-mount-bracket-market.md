<p><p>Rack mount brackets are categorized by their height in rack units (U), where 1U is 1.75 inches tall. The types include 1U, 2U, 3U, and 4U, with each additional U representing an increase in height. Typically, 1U brackets are used for smaller devices, while 2U and 3U accommodate slightly larger equipment, such as servers or networking gear. The 4U type is suitable for bulkier devices, and the "Others" category includes specialized or custom-sized brackets.</p></p>
