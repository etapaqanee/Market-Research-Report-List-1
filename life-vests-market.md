<p><p>The life vests market encompasses three primary types: foam vests, inflatable vests, and hybrid vests. Foam vests are made from buoyant materials, providing inherent flotation and durability, ideal for various water activities. Inflatable vests are lightweight and compact, offering buoyancy upon inflation, making them comfortable for prolonged use. Hybrid vests combine characteristics of both, featuring built-in flotation and the option to inflate for additional buoyancy, catering to diverse safety needs in recreational and professional marine environments.</p></p>
