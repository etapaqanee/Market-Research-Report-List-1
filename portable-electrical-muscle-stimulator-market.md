<p><p>The Portable Electrical Muscle Stimulator market comprises various types designed for specific therapeutic purposes. Neuromuscular Electrical Stimulation (NMES) targets muscle activation to improve strength and recovery. Functional Electrical Stimulation (FES) aids in movement and rehabilitation for patients with motor impairments. Transcutaneous Electrical Nerve Stimulation (TENS) provides pain relief by stimulating nerves. Interferential therapy delivers intersecting currents for deeper tissue pain management. Burst Mode Alternating Current mimics natural muscle activity, while Micro Current Electrical Neuro Muscular Stimulators (MENS) promote cellular repair. Other devices may include advanced technology for specialized applications.</p></p>
