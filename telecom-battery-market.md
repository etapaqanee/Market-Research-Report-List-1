<p><p>The telecom battery market primarily consists of two main types: lead-acid batteries and lithium-ion batteries. Lead-acid batteries are traditional power sources known for their reliability and cost-effectiveness, making them suitable for backup applications. However, they are heavier and have a shorter lifespan. In contrast, lithium-ion batteries offer higher energy density, longer cycle life, and faster charging, making them increasingly popular for modern telecom infrastructure. Both types play a critical role in ensuring uninterrupted connectivity and service reliability.</p></p>
