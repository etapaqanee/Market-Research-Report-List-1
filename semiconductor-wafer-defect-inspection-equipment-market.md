<p><strong>The Semiconductor Wafer Defect Inspection Equipment Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Needle Contact Method</li><li>Non Contact Method</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The semiconductor wafer defect inspection equipment market comprises two primary methods: the needle contact method and the non-contact method. The needle contact method involves physical probes to detect surface defects by making direct contact with the wafer, offering high accuracy but potential risk of damage. In contrast, the non-contact method utilizes optical or laser technologies to scan wafers without touching them, ensuring safety and enabling faster inspections. Each method has distinct advantages and applications based on the desired defect detection precision and wafer protection.</p></p>
