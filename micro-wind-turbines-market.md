<p><ul><li>Horizontal Axis Wind Turbine</li><li>Vertical Axis Wind Turbine</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The micro wind turbine market is primarily categorized into two types: Horizontal Axis Wind Turbines (HAWT) and Vertical Axis Wind Turbines (VAWT). HAWTs, characterized by their blades positioned horizontally and typically mounted on tall towers, are known for higher efficiency and are widely used in various applications. In contrast, VAWTs feature a vertical orientation, making them suitable for urban environments and areas with turbulent wind conditions. Both types serve diverse energy needs, promoting sustainable energy generation on a smaller scale.</p></p>
