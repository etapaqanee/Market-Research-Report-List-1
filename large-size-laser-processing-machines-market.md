<p><p>The Large Size Laser Processing Machines market is primarily categorized into two main types: CO2 Laser Processing Machines and Fiber Laser Processing Machines. CO2 lasers utilize a gas mixture, offering high-quality cutting and engraving on non-metal materials, making them ideal for applications in industries like textiles and wood. In contrast, Fiber Laser Processing Machines use a solid-state laser, enabling efficient cutting and welding of metals with superior precision and speed, catering to industries such as automotive and aerospace.</p></p>
