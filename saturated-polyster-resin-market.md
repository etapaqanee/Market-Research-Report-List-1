<p><p>The saturated polyester resin market is divided into two primary types: liquid and solid saturated polyester resins. Liquid saturated polyester resin is characterized by its fluid consistency, making it suitable for applications requiring easy application and penetration, such as coatings and adhesives. In contrast, solid saturated polyester resin is available in a more robust, solid form, suitable for applications like thermoplastics and molded products. Both types are essential in various industries, including automotive, construction, and electronics, due to their durability and versatility.</p></p>
