<p><p>The plastic films and sheets market encompasses various types, each serving unique applications. LDPE (Low-Density Polyethylene) and LLDPE (Linear Low-Density Polyethylene) are known for flexibility and moisture resistance, ideal for packaging. PA (Polyamide) offers strength and barrier properties for applications like food packaging. PVC (Polyvinyl Chloride) is versatile with excellent durability, while BOPP (Biaxially Oriented Polypropylene) is popular for its clarity and resistance, often used in food wrapping. "Others" include materials like PET and HDPE, catering to diverse industrial needs.</p></p>
