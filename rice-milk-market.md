<p><strong>The Rice Milk Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Powder Rice Milk</li><li>Fluid Rice Milk</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The rice milk market can be categorized into two main types: powder rice milk and fluid rice milk. Powder rice milk is a dehydrated form that requires reconstitution with water, offering convenience for storage and a long shelf life. It is often used in various recipes and by individuals seeking a portable dairy alternative. In contrast, fluid rice milk is a ready-to-drink beverage made from cooked rice and water, favored for its smooth texture and suitability for direct consumption or cooking.</p></p>
