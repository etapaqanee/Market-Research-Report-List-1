<p><p>The High Frequency Ultrasound Coagulating Cutter market is segmented into two primary types: monopolar and bipolar. Monopolar cutters use a single electrode to deliver energy, allowing for precise coagulation and cutting, making them suitable for larger tissue areas. In contrast, bipolar cutters employ two electrodes, generating energy directly between them, which enhances precision and minimizes tissue damage. Both technologies are prominent in surgical applications, offering advantages in hemostasis and tissue manipulation in minimally invasive procedures.</p></p>
