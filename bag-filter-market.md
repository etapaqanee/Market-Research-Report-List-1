<p><p>The bag filter market comprises several cleaning technologies, each catering to different industrial needs. Pulse-jet cleaning utilizes bursts of compressed air to dislodge dust from filter bags, ensuring efficient operation and minimal downtime. Reverse-air cleaning employs a reverse airflow to dislodge accumulated particles, making it suitable for high dust-load applications. Other methods may include mechanical shaking or water spray cleaning. Each type addresses specific filtration challenges, optimizing performance and extending the lifespan of the filters in various environments.</p></p>
