<p><strong>The Silicon Piezoresistive MEMS Pressure Sensor Market Analysis by types is segmented into:</strong></p>
<p><ul><li>Absolute Pressure Sensors</li><li>Relative (Gauge) Pressure Sensors</li><li>Differential Pressure Sensors</li></ul></p>
<p><p>The Silicon Piezoresistive MEMS Pressure Sensor market comprises three main types: Absolute Pressure Sensors, which measure pressure relative to a perfect vacuum; Relative (Gauge) Pressure Sensors, which gauge pressure against atmospheric pressure; and Differential Pressure Sensors, designed to measure the difference between two pressure points. These sensors leverage silicon piezoresistive technology to provide high sensitivity and accuracy, making them essential in various applications such as automotive, aerospace, and industrial processes, ensuring precise pressure monitoring and control.</p></p>
