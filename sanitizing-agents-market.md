<p><p>The sanitizing agents market is categorized into two main types: non-oxidizing and oxidizing disinfectants. Non-oxidizing disinfectants include subcategories such as alcohols, aldehydes, amphoterics, phenolics, and quaternary ammonium compounds (QACs), which work by disrupting microbial cell functions. Oxidizing disinfectants, such as peracetic acid and hydrogen peroxide, eliminate pathogens through oxidative reactions. Each type serves specific applications across healthcare, food processing, and household cleaning, ensuring effective microbial control and enhanced hygiene standards.</p></p>
